Who are we?

The NFC Ring was the brain child of John McLear, an inventor from Bradford, West Yorkshire in England.

He developed hundreds of ring prototypes and then took his idea to Kickstarter with a £30k goal.

This goal was smashed, hitting over £240k over 30 days! We now have a small team and the first batch of rings are in currently in production at our factory.
