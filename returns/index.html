Our returns and refund policy was jointly written by the NFC Ring community and approved by McLear Limited. Each change and previous versions are stored and each revision is timestamped to ensure document fidelity and integrity.

Non-obvious Terms used

Ring Bearer = Human that purchased a ring from McLear Ltd
McLear Ltd = Company that makes the ring.

Replacements

Circumstances under which replacements are required:

  • The ring purchased is the wrong size.
  • The ring purchased is the wrong color.
  • The ring purchased does not function with their target device (Phone/Tablet/Door lock). (Target device must be NFC compatible.)
  • The ring bearer has been in possession of the ring for less than 30 days and wants to change their ring.
  • A ring supplied by McLear Ltd has developed a fault within a 12 month period and there are no signs of foul play. Kickstarter backers will get a 2 year warranty, however we can only provide one "like for like" replacement, provided we have one in stock. Any ‘like for like’ replacements that we do not have in stock will be from our available range from our store.
  • A ring supplied by McLear Ltd does not fulfill our Quality Assurance criteria

The Replacement Process

  • The ring bearer should contact support@nfcring.com in the first instance, providing information on their current ring, their problem and what size/style they would like to exchange to.
  • The ring bearer should be offered a choice of:
    • Purchasing an additional second ring and being refunded when their original ring arrives with us.
    • Purchasing an additional second ring at a heavily discounted rate.
    • Sending their current ring back and receiving a replacement ring in due course.
  • Should a similar color size ring option not be immediately available, the ring bearer should be offered a ring of the same value that is available on the NFC Ring online store.
  • The ring bearer should send the faulty ring back to the UK/US distribution point.
    • United States of America / Canada should be sent to "Returns @ McLear Ltd, 158 evergreen rd, boulder creek, CA 95006, United States"
    • Rest of the world orders should be sent to "Returns @ McLear Ltd, Newby School House, Ryan Street, Bradford, West Yorkshire, BD5 7DQ, United Kingdom"
    • In the packaging please include your email address, any order documentation you have and your preferred size/sku also a return address
    • Please include your name and returns address so it is clearly visible on the back of your package
  • The ring bearer should always be notified of replacement ring lead time.
  • The ring returned by the ring bearer must be in new condition (No marks or scratches).

Admin Fees Required

If you ordered the wrong size ring, provided an incorrect/incomplete address, or would like to change the style of ring you ordered, we require a non-refundable fee of £5/$5 per ring to cover our admin and shipping cost overheads.

This can be paid online at http://store.nfcring.com/products/admin-shipping-fee or for USA/Canada/Mexico orders - http://americas.nfcring.com/products/admin-shipping-fee

Please add a note to your order referencing your previous order number (or name/email if you were a Kickstarter backer).

Refunds

Circumstances under which refunds are required:

  • The ring bearer has been in possession of the ring for less than 30 days and the ring is in "like new" condition.
  • The ring has developed a fault within a 12 month period and there are no obvious signs of foul play.
  • The ring does not fulfill the Quality Assurance criteria - https://github.com/JohnMcLear/nfc-ring-specs/wiki/Quality-Assurance-Procedure
  • The ring bearer hasn't received their ring and has contacted us within 3 months of placing their order to notify us.

The Refund Process

  • The ring bearer should send the faulty ring back to the UK/US distribution point.
  • If requested, a replacement ring will be sent out free of charge at the time of receipt - or else a refund should be issued. Note: Refunds via bank will be possible for pre-orders and orders (not for crowd-sourced orders)
    • Should a ring bearer send a ring back and it not be received by McLear Ltd, tracking information should be provided that provides proof of shipping.
  • United States of America / Canada should be sent to "Returns @ McLear Ltd, 158 Evergreen Rd, Boulder Creek, CA 95006, United States"
  • Rest of the world orders should be sent to "Returns @ McLear Ltd, Newby School House, Ryan Street, Bradford, West Yorkshire, BD5 7DQ, United Kingdom"
  • In the packaging please include your email address, any order documentation you have and your preferred size/sku also a return address

Questions / Scenarios:

  • What if the app doesn't work with my phone?
    The ring can be returned for a refund
  • What if the app stops working after 30 days?
    The NFC Ring unlock app comes with a warranty like any software you purchase. This warranty is limited to the verification and ring registration functionality of the app.
  • What if I lose my ring but need exactly the same ring inlays?
    There is nothing McLear Ltd can do about this. You will have to purchase a replacement ring.
  • Is there any possibility to organize back shipping from UK, for example in Switzerland where the shipment costs more than a new ring?
    Sadly no, maybe in the future when we expand we will find a way to make this feasible, but for now the best we can offer is shipping back to the UK or the US.
  • Is there a problem with overloading the ring involuntary during checkout (like the shops do with the RFID security tags)?
    TLDR; No. (See previous document revisions for full explanation.)
  • How should I package and ship my ring for return?
    Package your ring in a strong jiffy bag that wont split in shipping. Ship your ring tracked. If your ring fails to arrive with us there is very little we can do to help you.
